SALAD VERTE

INGREDIENTS FOR VINAIGRETTE

  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 tsp English or French mustard
  • 1 tsp red wine vinegar
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

INGREDIENTS FOR SALAD

  • 2 lettuces (Romaine, Little Gem)
  • handful of salad leaves (watercress, rocket or baby spinach)
  • handful of soft fresh herbs (tarragon, parsley or chives)
